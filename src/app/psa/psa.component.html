PUBLIC SERVICE ANNOUNCEMENTS

Our Public Service Announcement (PSA) resources are messages in the public interest that are designed to be disseminated without charge, and with the objective of raising awareness of, and improving public attitudes and behavior towards, parenting during the COVID-19 pandemic and beyond.

If you can't find a PSA resource available in your language, please send us an email at: translate@covid19parenting.com

Please note that these translations were not created by the World Health Organization (WHO), and WHO is not responsible for the content or accuracy of these translations .
