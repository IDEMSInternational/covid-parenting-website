TIPS FOR CASEWORKERS

We have designed special parenting tip sheets to be used by social workers for behavioral parent training, to help parents build positive parent-child relationships through play, reinforcing positive and managing difficult behaviours, creating structure and routines, talking about COVID-19, keeping children safe online, and reducing stress and conflict.

If you can't find a caseworker tip sheet available in your language, please send us an email at: translate@covid19parenting.com

Please note that these translations were not created by the World Health Organization (WHO), and WHO is not responsible for the content or accuracy of these translations.
